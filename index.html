where to find an ethercache.

How do you know if you’ve found an ethercache?

Mostly, you’ll feel it in your heart.

What does an ethercache look like? Ethercashes look like QR codes that look like the one on the right. They are about 6 inches by 6 inches and hidden in public places (as marked on the map above).

If you find one, it’s your lucky day. Scan its QR code to play.

What does this have to do “with the ether”?

Ethercache visitor books are stored in a smart contract on Ethereum. When you write a message in an ethercache visitor book, that message is stored on Ethereum, making you forever a part of the ethercache history.

What do you need to play?

You’ll need a mobile browser that’s Ethereum enabled such as Cipher.
